VICTORIA XU 许星程

Clocking in at 5’4”, Victoria Xu is the perfect compromise between size and power. With a focus on Art and Computer Science, she passionately creates and experiments across mediums from coding to painting and places from LA to Charlotte to Providence. Her skills and work experience include animation, web platforms, and illustrations. Victoria works tirelessly to push the boundaries of what’s possible in the intersection of Art and Computer Science, with a unique talent for interdisciplinary visual storytelling.
